01. About

I'm a Computer Engineering student at McMaster University working at the intersection of hardware and software. My focus is firmware and embedded systems — writing the low-level code that makes hardware come alive, and designing the boards it runs on.

Through robotics teams and personal projects I've worked across the full stack of an embedded product: PCB design in Altium, communication protocols like CAN, SPI, I²C and UART, sensor integration and validation, and higher-level work in computer vision and machine learning.

I'm looking for opportunities in firmware, embedded systems, robotics, and software engineering where I can ship real hardware.
