## Overview

A web app for promoting healthier lifestyles, built at **DeltaHacks 2025**. Users can
scan a photo of their meal and instantly receive its nutritional values and name, plus
personalized recipes and diet guidance from an AI assistant.

## Features

- **Meal scanning** — an image-processing model identifies food from a photo and returns full nutritional data
- **Personalized coaching** — a Cohere-powered assistant responds based on the user's diet and goals
- **Recipes & nutrition** — meal suggestions with the numbers to back them up

## What I worked on

- Trained / integrated the Cohere model for personalized diet responses
- Built the image-processing pipeline for meal recognition
- Connected the model outputs to a clean web front end
