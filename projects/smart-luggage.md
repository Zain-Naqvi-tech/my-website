## Overview

A team-built system that transports luggage safely and efficiently from an elevated
point down to a lower level. It combines custom **3D-printed parts**, **laser-cut gears**,
and a **rotary actuator** for smooth motion, with a **QArm** robotic arm programmed to
automate scanning and sorting.

## What it does

- Moves luggage between two elevation levels reliably and repeatably
- Uses a QArm to scan and sort items automatically
- Custom mechanical design: 3D-printed mounts and laser-cut gear train
